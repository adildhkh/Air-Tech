WHAT WE DO

STELLA®
SATCOM Technology of Elaborate Luneburg Lens Antenna

We invented a state-of-the-art antenna system STELLA®, a satellite communication antenna product in Low Earth Orbit (LEO-Satcom) with a device as small as a golf ball size for the Q-band frequency (33 – 50GHz). Our X-band antenna device, 8 – 12GHz, with operation extension going beyond the Ku-band (up to 20GHz) is less than a basket ball size.

Our Antenna Technology is Unique

The unique features of the patent pending STELLA® antenna system include an extremely wide scanning angle capability and a blanket coverage of the sky or the Earth with no dead zone in the electromagnetics wave illumination pattern. STELLA® has 360 degree in azimuth and 180 degree in elevation scanning capability, a simultaneous connection and tracking of several LEO (Low Earth Orbit) satellites

STELLA contribution to Satcom technology

USAT’s contribution to the advancement of SATCOM technology will be undeniable and impactful. STELLA’s® 3D printed lens antenna is a low-cost production device. It will modestly promote the proliferation of Satcom internet and 5G mobile cellular phone. The direct addressable market of STELLA® are the Low Earth Orbit communication applications of SATCOM including IoT (Internet-of-Things).
